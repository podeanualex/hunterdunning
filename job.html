    Our client is an internationally renowned, multi award winning and progressive Architectural Studio in Central London. Their office has built a strong reputation for its work in the luxury market and currently manages a number of prestigious projects in London within commercial, high-end residential, mixed-use, Masterplanning and Educational projects. Our client is intent on producing exceptional architecture that stands the test of time, working across geographies, building sizes and typologies.

    As part of the next planned stage of growth and development; a fantastic new opportunity has now been created for a talented Project Architect to join our client's London office on a permanent and full time basis. Working directly and closely with the Managing Directors, the successful candidate will be responsible for the project running of a number of exceptional projects currently in the studio. You will be required to lead on the design and delivery of some outstanding UK and Swiss projects and also assist on a number of leisure, very high end private residential and prime commercial projects. It will be your responsibility to ensure the delivery of design excellence across the portfolio of their projects, from conception through to completion. You will also be required to delegate and lead the team in projects, and also engage with the junior members of staff.

    In order to apply for this role, you must be able to demonstrate:

    • A strong ability to work across all RIBA work stages.
    • An in-depth understanding of technical issues and familiarity UK Building regulations and specifications.
    • A strong ability to demonstrate an understanding of on-site construction , technical detailing, and a knowledge of procurement routes
    • A very strong proficiency in AutoCAD, Adobe suite, Rhino will also be required.
    • Strong abilities in your visual communication and design skills will be advantageous, preferably with strong free hand sketches.

    Applicants for this role will have excellent organisational skills, a strong attention to detail and have a pro-active and positive disposition. You will be good at relationship building with the ability to liaise at all levels. You will need to be a practical and strategic thinker who is self-motivated and have the ability to work autonomously.

    Our client is specifically interested in ambitious characters that have worked on a lot of projects, but are now ready to take the next step up and want to take full ownership of their work, and also run a small team. Ideally, someone that is hungry for more, and ready for the next level in their career.

    They are a relatively young company, having been established in 2005, so the successful candidate has the possibility to become an integral part of the practice as they grow in size â€“ becoming a key player in the senior management team.

    This is a fantastic opportunity to join a rapidly expanding business with a portfolio of highly prestigious projects. In addition to an excellent salary the successful candidate will also be offered an unrivalled salary and fully comprehensive package to secure the right candidate for this role. There is a generous benefits package on offer.
